Strategy Making × Heuristic Decision Making

This is a list of articles from the intersection of Strategy Making and Heuristic Decision Making.

  • Artinger F, Petersen M, Gingerenzer G, Weibler G. 2015. Heuristics as adaptive strategies in management. Journal of Organizational Behavior 36(S1): 33-52.
  • Gavetti G, Rivkin JW. 2007. On the origin of strategy: action and cognition over time. Organization Science 18(3): 420-439.
  • Hambrick DC, Mason PA. 1984. Upper echelons: the organization as a reflection of its top managers. Academy of Management Review 9(2): 193-206.
  • Hambrick DC. 2007. Upper echelons theory: an update. Academy of Management Review 32(2): 334-343.
  • Kahneman D, Tversky A. 1979. An analysis of decision under risk. Econometrica, 47: 263-291.
  • Ocasio W, Joseph J. 2005. An attention-based theory of strategy formulation: linking micro- and macroperspectives in strategy processes. Advances in Strategic Management 22: 39-61.
  • Tversky A, Kahneman D. 1974. Judgment under uncertainty: heuristics and biases. Science 185(4157): 1124-1131.
